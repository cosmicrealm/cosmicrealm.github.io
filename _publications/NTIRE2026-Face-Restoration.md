Abstract:
This paper reviews the NTIRE 2026 real-world face restoration challenge, summarizing the task, evaluation protocol, participating teams, submitted methods, and final results. The challenge focuses on perceptual quality, realism, and identity consistency for real-world degraded face restoration.
