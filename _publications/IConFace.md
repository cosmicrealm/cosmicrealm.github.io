Abstract:
IConFace is a unified reference-aware and no-reference face restoration framework. It distills same-identity references into a reliability-weighted global identity anchor while using the degraded image as the spatial structure anchor, enabling one checkpoint to exploit references when available and perform degraded-only restoration when they are absent.
